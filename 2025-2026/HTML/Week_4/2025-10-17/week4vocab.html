Vocabulary Tags

<imag>

Displays an image on the page.

<div>

Groups content into blocks.

<a>

Create a hyperlink.

<hr>

Draws a horizontal line to separate sections.

<span>

Highlights or style parts of text without breaking lines.

<p>

Creates a paragraph of text.

<br>

breaks the line and make the text continue on the text.

id

identify a unique element within the page.

class

give the same style or behavior to multiple elements at the same time.

<h1>

Displays the most important heading.
